History

I.Love.Butter is a well-known eatery in Sydney, Australia, famous for its unique approach to modern Australian cuisine. Established in 2017 by Chef Sam Smith and business partner Emily Brown, the restaurant quickly gained a reputation for its innovative dishes and laid-back atmosphere.

The concept behind I.Love.Butter was to create a dining experience that celebrated the simple pleasures of food with a focus on high-quality ingredients and bold flavors. The menu often features a fusion of classic and contemporary dishes, with an emphasis on fresh, locally-sourced produce.
